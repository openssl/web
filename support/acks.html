Sponsor Acknowledgements

The OpenSSL project depends on volunteer efforts and financial support from the end user community. That support comes in the form of donations, contracts, and volunteer contributions. Since all of these activities support the continued development and improvement of OpenSSL, we consider all of them to be sponsors of the OpenSSL project.

We would like to identify and thank the following such sponsors for their past or current significant support of the OpenSSL project. Except as noted sponsors are listed within categories in order of overall contribution value. Please note that we ask permission to identify sponsors and that some sponsors we consider eligible for inclusion here have requested to remain anonymous.
